Platforms

Platforms you could publish your works on.

Primary Choice

AO3

The most popular platform for both Thai and foreign writers. The gold standard FanFiction. Excellent community feedback, easy-to-use interface, and great monetization options for creators.

ReadAWrite

Very popular amoung Thai Writers & Readers. Easy to use, and has many features.

#Global #Fanfic #Archive

Wattpad

Great for original stories and reaching a massive younger audience. Perfect for Romance and YA genres.

#Global #Originals #Community

Dek-D

The legendary Thai platform for teenagers and student writers. Strong roots in light novels and fantasy.

#Thai #Classic #TeenCulture

Medium

If you write essays, non-fiction, or poetic prose, this is the cleanest place to share your thoughts.

#Articles #NonFiction #Clean
